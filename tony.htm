Tony Mucci

tony@verse3.com

Long Island, NY

Drums/backup vocals

At a very young age I was inspired to play the drums from the "Little Drummer Boy" Christmas cartoon. I headed straight for the band room in my grade school! There was a very supportive drum teacher in the school band that taught me to never give up and practice always pays off. I continued to play through grade school and in Jr. High. I will never forget that Christmas morning when my parents "oops Santa clause" had a red sparkle CB 700 drum set, I knew where I belonged. Neighborhood musicians inspired my style of singing and drumming such as: my sister Marie Mucci, SLD band, Pete Corona and Rudy Sabatini. I have very loving and supportive family and friends. I have always had a faith through God, but not until 10 years ago did I truly understand why we are on this place they call earth. Only a year ago did I learn what path the Lord has chosen for me. I have been watching my younger brother progress rapidly in his musical talents. I told him now all we have to do is pray for a singer and a guitar player. *warning: Be careful of what you pray for. You may just get it. The Lord sent a very quiet musician named Robbie. He came over to our house where Jason and I would practice. I could hardly believe the sound the very first time we sat down to play. We continued writing and playing songs inspired by the Lord for about a year, and before we realized it we were Verse: 3. I hope you enjoy the musical message we have to offer. God has a plan.

Tony
