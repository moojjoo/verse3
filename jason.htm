Jason "Jay" Mucci

jason@verse3.com

Long Island, NY

Bass/Backup Vocals

I have always been inspired to play. Since an early childhood my brother and I have spent time playing and listening to music together. My first instrument of choice were the drums, but once I saw a crazy bass guitarist named Flea from a band called the Red Hot Chilli Peppers I was hooked. Tony had a great influence on me and it didn't take long before I was performing for him and his friends. As I grew older and with moving to North Carolina I put my music on hold for awhile. My relationship with God has always been there. It may not have been the closest at times but all that changed when Tony moved down here to N.C. Not only did he show me the light, but he awoke my musical calling as well. So, now I mixed the two in my life; the wild crazy bass bangin' guitarist with a positive praise and worship style. Some of the bands I listen to are D.C. Talks, Jars of Clay, P.O.D., Blindside, and of course my all time favorite, hands down the best band ever CREED. They have had a big influence on our sound and it really shows in new song One Last Stand in which I was kinda going for that Creed sound. I have something to say to all you kids my age out there, don't ever believe that you are too young or not good enough to succeed in music. If you have faith you can succeed in anything you do. ROCK ON!

Jason Mucci
